Your Project Journey

Every step,
thoughtfully guided.

01 Project Reserved

Your signed agreement and project deposit officially reserve your place in my schedule.

02 Content & Image Collection

I’ll review your current website, photographs, pricing, testimonials, inspiration, and any notes you want considered.

Your part: Send selected files or share a Pixieset gallery link. You do not need to organize everything perfectly first.
03 Homepage Direction

I’ll create the initial homepage direction so we can align on the visual style, structure, and overall feeling.

Your part: Review the design and send one clear, organized round of feedback.
04 Direction Approved

Once you are happy with the design direction, I’ll prepare to build the approved look across your Pixieset website.

Your part: At this stage, we’ll arrange secure Pixieset access. Please do not email your password.
05 Full Pixieset Build

I’ll complete the remaining pages, mobile layouts, image placement, written refinements, and relevant booking links or built-in Pixieset features.

06 Refine & Launch

We’ll complete the included revision rounds, review the full website on desktop and mobile, test the important links, and prepare everything for launch.

Your part: Send final revision notes together in one message so the last refinements stay smooth and efficient.
